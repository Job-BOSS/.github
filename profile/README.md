# Job BOSS Platform

**Job BOSS Platform** is a career management application designed to organize and track employment opportunities efficiently. As a comprehensive **Job BOSS Toolkit**, it centralizes all job applications, interview dates, company research, and follow-up tasks in one secure location. This **Job BOSS Organizer** automatically reminds you of application deadlines and scheduled interviews while maintaining detailed notes for each position. With **Job BOSS Tracker**, you can monitor offer statuses, compare compensation packages, and store contact information for recruiters and hiring managers.

## Features

- **Application Tracking**: The **Job BOSS Platform** logs every submitted application with company details.
- **Interview Management**: Use **Job BOSS Toolkit** to schedule and prepare for upcoming interviews.
- **Offer Comparison**: As a **Job BOSS Organizer**, it compares salaries, benefits, and growth opportunities.
- **Document Storage**: This **Job BOSS Tracker** securely stores resumes, cover letters, and portfolios.
